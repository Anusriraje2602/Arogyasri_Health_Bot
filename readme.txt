# Aarogya Sri

AI-Powered Multilingual Health Assistant

Trusted healthcare guidance — for every language, every home.

## Problem

Millions of Indians struggle to access reliable healthcare information due to:

- Language barriers
- Healthcare misinformation
- High healthcare costs
- Long hospital waiting times
- Limited digital health literacy

## Solution

Aarogya Sri is an AI-powered multilingual health assistant that provides:

- Voice and text-based interaction
- Local language support
- Verified health guidance
- Preventive healthcare tips
- Government healthcare scheme awareness
- Low-bandwidth accessibility

## Target Users

- Rural and semi-urban populations
- Non-English speakers
- Daily wage workers
- Farmers
- Low and middle-income families
- Entry-level smartphone users

## Key Features

### Multilingual AI Assistant
Users can ask health-related questions in their preferred language.

### Voice Interaction
Speech-to-text and text-to-speech support for accessibility.

### Verified Health Information
Provides trusted guidance while avoiding diagnosis or prescriptions.

### Government Scheme Awareness
Helps users discover affordable healthcare programs and subsidies.

### Personalized Wellness Tips
Daily preventive healthcare guidance.

## User Flow

1. Select preferred language
2. Ask a health question
3. AI asks clarifying questions
4. Verified guidance is provided
5. Next steps are suggested

## Safety Boundaries

The system does NOT:

- Diagnose diseases
- Prescribe medicines
- Handle emergencies
- Replace professional medical advice

- Medical Accuracy ≥ 85%
- Hallucination Rate < 5%
